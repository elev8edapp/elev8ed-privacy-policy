Delete Your Elev8ed Account

If you wish to delete your Elev8ed account and all associated data, you can do so directly from within the app.

📱 Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Elev8ed app on your device.
  2. Go to the settings section.
  3. Tap on “Delete My Account”.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

🧹 What Happens When You Delete Your Account

  • Your account data (email, habits, goals, vision statements, etc.) will be permanently deleted from our Firebase database.
  • Your authentication credentials will be removed via Firebase Authentication.
  • Deleted data cannot be recovered.

⏳ Retention

We do not retain any user data after account deletion. All personal and usage data is erased immediately.

📧 Need Help?

If you cannot access the app or have questions, contact us at elev8ed.app1@gmail.com and we will manually assist with account deletion.
